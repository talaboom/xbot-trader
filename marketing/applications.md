- Technology-based innovation ✓
- Developing a new product for market ✓

### How to Apply
1. **Call Monday: 1-877-994-4727** (toll-free)
2. Tell them: "I'm a Canadian tech entrepreneur, I've built an AI-powered fintech SaaS product that's live and deployed. I'm looking for IRAP funding to continue R&D and expand to market."
