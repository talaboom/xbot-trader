# Website plan — Shopify build

Goal: a live, professional-looking store at `roseoilcanada.com` (or whatever domain) in **under one week**, capable of handling both retail DTC sales (5 mL / 30 mL bottles) and wholesale B2B inquiries (compounding pharmacies, cosmetics formulators).

**Why Shopify and not a custom site:**
- Live in a day, not a month
- Handles payments, tax, shipping, inventory out of the box
- Has native wholesale features (at $89/mo Advanced plan; also doable with a third-party wholesale app at Basic plan)
- Every Canadian indie beauty brand uses Shopify — their buyers are already familiar

**Recommended plan:** Shopify Basic ($38 CAD/mo year-1), upgrade to Shopify ($105 CAD/mo) once monthly revenue crosses ~$5K.

---

## Setup in order (aim for 4–6 hours total the first day)

### Hour 1 — account + domain

1. Sign up at shopify.com (14-day free trial, no credit card required)
2. Buy domain `roseoilcanada.com` (or alternatives below if taken) — Shopify's domain registration is fine, $18 CAD/year
   - If .com taken: `roseoilcanada.ca` is Canadian and actually preferable
   - Alternatives: `bulgarianroseoil.ca`, `kazanlakrose.ca`, `marianaroseoil.ca`
3. Set store currency to CAD
4. Set tax region: Canada, enable automatic HST/GST calculation by province

### Hour 2 — theme

**Recommended theme:** `Dawn` (free, built-in, clean/modern) or `Craft` (free, more artisanal feel, good for story-driven brands). Don't pay for a premium theme in year one.

- Pick `Craft` for the story-heavy Bulgarian heritage angle
- Upload logo (commission a designer at Fiverr for ~$80 CAD, 48-hour turnaround — specify "botanical, clean, Bulgarian rose, serif wordmark")
- Set brand colors: dusty rose (#C7948B) primary, deep forest green (#2D4A3E) accent, warm cream (#F5EFE6) background

### Hour 3 — product pages

Create three products at launch:

1. **Bulgarian Rose Oil — 5 mL** (retail)
2. **Bulgarian Rose Oil — 30 mL** (retail / prosumer formulator)
3. **Bulgarian Rose Oil — 100 mL** (professional / small wholesale)

Later add: **Bulk 1 kg** as a hidden / password-gated product for wholesale-approved accounts only.

See `Product copy` section below for ready-to-paste text.

### Hour 4 — shipping

Canada-wide shipping:
- Canada Post Expedited Parcel (2–9 business days): $15–30 per order
- Canada Post Xpresspost (1–2 business days): $30–50 per order
- Free shipping on orders > $200 CAD
- USA: enable international shipping at $35–60 flat rate. Don't enable Europe/rest-of-world yet (cosmetic import rules vary too much).

Set up Shopify Shipping so labels print in one click.

### Hour 5 — pages

- **Our Story** — Bulgarian heritage, Mariana's background, the Kazanlak Valley
- **Our Quality** — GC-MS, PDO certification, producer-direct relationship (this is the trust page, link prominently from every product page)
- **Wholesale** — B2B landing page with inquiry form (see copy below)
- **Contact**
- **FAQ**
- **Shipping & Returns**
- **Terms** + **Privacy Policy** — use Shopify's free generators

### Hour 6 — apps + integrations

**Must-have (all free or very cheap):**
- Shopify Email (free for first 10K emails/month)
- Klaviyo (free under 250 contacts, then scales) — better long-term email marketing
- **Wholesale Gorilla** or **Shopify B2B** (built-in at Plus plan, otherwise ~$49/mo) — creates a separate pricing tier for approved wholesale customers
- Google Analytics 4 via Shopify's native integration
- **Trust Badges** (free apps; adds GC-MS / PDO / "made by hand" badges to product pages)

**Nice-to-have (add later):**
- Loox or Judge.me for product reviews (free tier OK)
- Shogun page builder (only if you want fancier landing pages; skip for year 1)

Genuine Bulgarian rose oil is one of the most falsified ingredients in the cosmetic industry. Over 80% of oil sold online as "Bulgarian rose" is geranium oil cut with synthetic fragrance compounds.

Ours is not.

This oil is steam-distilled from *Rosa damascena* petals harvested at dawn in May and June in the Kazanlak Valley — a region in central Bulgaria that has produced the world's most prized rose oil for over 400 years. ## Our Story page copy

### Heading
From the Kazanlak Rose Valley to Canada

### Body

My name is Mariana, and I was born in Bulgaria — in a village about 40 minutes from Kazanlak, the valley that has produced roses for the world's perfume houses since before Chanel No. 5 existed.

I left Bulgaria as a young woman, studied pharmacy, and spent 30 years in Canada — eventually as president of Cami Pharmaceutical in Mississauga. I ran a pharmaceutical company, but I never stopped thinking about the roses.

Every year, for about four weeks in May and June, the Kazanlak Valley fills with a scent that doesn't exist anywhere else. Families that have been distilling rose oil for five, six, seven generations work from 4 AM to midnight, because the petals must reach the still within hours of being picked. It takes roughly **4,000 kilograms of rose petals to make one kilogram of rose oil.** That is the real reason it is so expensive — and the real reason so much of what is sold as Bulgarian rose oil is fake.

I spent the last two years rebuilding my connections with the producers in my region — including one small distillery that has been in the same family for five generations, and that I know personally. In 2026, I started importing their oil to Canada.

I only sell oil I would put in my own mother's skincare. Every batch is tested twice — at origin, and again on Canadian soil — using gas chromatography–mass spectrometry, the same technique the big fragrance houses use. I publish the lab reports. I publish the lot numbers. I'd rather sell less of something real than more of something fake.

Welcome. Thank you for being here.

## First 90 days post-launch (traffic + conversion plan)

1. **Google Ads on "Bulgarian rose oil Canada" and variants** — low competition keyword, $200–400 CAD/month budget, should generate 10–30 qualified visitors/month and 0.5–2 sales.
2. **Instagram content** — 3 posts/week: process photos (distillation, petals, lab), Mariana's story, customer-use (aromatherapist spotlight). Goal: 500 followers in 90 days.
3. **Email capture** — 10% first-order discount for email signup. Goal: 500 emails in 90 days.
4. **Press outreach** — pitch 5 Canadian clean-beauty publications (Reversed, Chatelaine, Best Health, FASHION, Cult Beauty Canada) with a press kit. Goal: 1 feature.
5. **Faire passive orders** — target 2–5 B2B orders from Faire buyers in 90 days without active outreach.
6. **Cold outreach** — 30 emails/month from `CUSTOMERS.md` list.

Realistic year-one revenue from the website alone: **$25,000–50,000 CAD** assuming 4 kg imported. The wholesale pipeline through Faire + cold outreach does the rest.
